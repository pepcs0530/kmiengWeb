LED Explosionproof Light

Dramatic advances in technology for LED luminaires broadened the applicability of this type of illumination, creating an exciting new option for hazardous, industrial and other highly demanding locations. Compared to traditional light sources, LED luminaires can deliver longer life, enhanced energy efficiency, greater eco-friendliness, lowered maintenance demands and equal or better quality of light.
