COMPANY PROFILE

History

KMI Enginnering (Thailand) Co., Ltd. was established in 1996, with an initial registered capital of 30.311 thousand USD (1 million THB). With experienced consultant engineers to support the Mining Plant and Transformers industries in Thailand and Laos. From this position we were recognize the opportunity for further possibility of expanding company objective field, we began to become an importer and after services provider through present, 2020.
